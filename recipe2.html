Vampire Mist Cocktail

  • Prep time 0:10
  • Yields 4servings

Ingredients

  • 6 oz vanilla vodka
  • 8 oz gingerbeer
  • 6 oz cranberry juice
  • 4 oz pomegranate juice
  • Food grade dry ice

Directions

  1. Step 1:Mix all ingredients (except for dry ice) in a pitcher.
  2. Turn glasses upside down and press rim on wet paper towel. Dip moistened glasses in red sugar.
  3. Step 2:Pour drink into individual ice-filled glasses. Place a small piece of dry ice into each glass (using tongs). Drink will bubble and mist for about 5 minutes.
  4. Step 3:Do not drink until all dry ice has melted. This should be easy to avoid, since dry ice sinks and stays at the bottom of glass.
  5. Step 4:For Mocktail: Omit vodka and add additional ginger beer.
